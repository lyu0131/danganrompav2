About

This non‑commercial fan site aims to capture the tension, humor, and theatrical chaos of Trigger Happy Havoc through interactive UI motifs — the Truth‑Bullet dial, a crosshair cursor with a muzzle‑flash click, and a neon‑noir palette that swings between hope and despair.
