Game Play

Danganronpa is a visual novel type role playing mystery solving game. Developed by Spike Chunsoft, a Japanese game development company, Danganronpa was first released on november 25th, 2010. the game quickly gained popularity and was localized to be sold in different countries. Today, the Danganronpa series now has 3 main series games, 1 spin off game, multiple small games, and 3 anime series.
